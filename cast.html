Idea about creating a game was invented in 2010. From the very beginning the game was intended for the PlayStation 3 at PlayStation Move. But player didn't like the idea about realising the game with PlayStation Move. And company decided to recreate a game in new genre line interactive movie on PlayStation 4, because it's about to release. The game was created in game engine named Decima. The scenatist Larry Fessenden (who also played a Stranger in Until Dawn) and Gram Reznick writted over 10 000 pages of scenario. To increase the player's agency, the team envisioned a system named the "butterfly effect". Every choice the player makes in the game, it changes the whole story and ultimately leads to different endings. About how SuperMassive Games created a graphic, story and characters with actors, you can find out at the following link: Behind the scenes of Until Dawn (2015).
