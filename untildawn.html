Supermassive Games is a british game studio, they're were founded in 2008 in Guildford, Great Britain. Many games that they developed is interactive horrors like Until Dawn (2015), The Quarry (2022), Dark Pictures Anthology consists of 4 games - Man of Medan (2019), Little Hope (2020), House of Ashes (2021), Devil In Me (2022). In 2022 Supermassive Games was fully acquired by Nordisk Games, part of the Egmont Group, and we look forward to even more exciting times with them alongside us. Almost all games can be played on PS4,PS5 or PC. In studio of the company is working over 300 people.
